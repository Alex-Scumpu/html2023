Beautiful performance

A striking front that exudes power, effortlessly flowing coupé lines and a dominantly wide stance - welcome to the RS 5 Coupé.

  • Welcome to the cockpit

    The command centre of the Audi RS 5 Coupé exudes exclusivity. Elegant heated front Super Sport seats with RS embossing in fine Nappa leather - alongside the sport contour leather steering wheel with paddle shifts continue the sophistication into the interior.

  • Powerful drive you can feel

    Powered by a 2.9-litre TFSI twin-turbo V6 engine with 450 PS, the RS 5 Coupé embodies high performance in impressive form. The RS exhaust system provides a thrilling sound experience in the process. Quattro all-wheel drive, with sport differential, also offers reliable traction for impressive driving dynamics and agile handling. The optional RS sport suspension plus with Dynamic Ride Control (DRC) enhances cornering performance and driving stability, providing an all-round enjoyable driving experience.

  • Characteristically coupé, resoundingly RS.

    The sporty silhouette of the RS 5 Coupé is visible at a first glance. The vehicle’s wide track is underscored by the flared wheel arches and the striking RS side sills. The robust rear with RS-specific diffuser insert and 19-inch 10-spoke star alloy wheels round off the dynamic impression. Personalise your RS 5 Coupé further with additional sporty elements. Choose from the optional carbon roof, making the vehicle more agile and dynamic due to its lightweight design, or open up new perspectives with the panoramic glass sunroof with adjustable electric sun shield on the RS 5 Coupé Vorsprung.
